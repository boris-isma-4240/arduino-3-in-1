Our goals.

Why We Chose the “Smart Village” Project.:

In today’s fast-paced world, people are often too busy to take proper care of their homes — checking if the windows are closed, watering plants, or managing everyday maintenance tasks.
That’s why we chose to work on the Smart Village project. Our idea was to design a village that can take care of itself, where technology handles routine tasks, and the user simply controls everything through an app.
With this vision in mind, we focused on creating autonomous house prototypes that require minimal human involvement.
Another important aspect of our project is energy efficiency. As electricity costs continue to rise, we wanted to find a more sustainable solution. That’s why we integrated a solar power station into our village concept — one that can supply clean energy to multiple homes.
This combination of smart automation and renewable energy makes our “Smart Village” not only convenient, but also future-ready.

Project information.

How it works?:

Our project is divided into three main parts: the Keyestudio Smart Home Kit, the Keyestudio Smart Farm Kit, and the Keyestudio Solar Station. Each part plays a unique role in building an autonomous and energy-efficient “Smart Village.”

  • The Smart Home Kit simulates a self-sustaining house equipped with automated features such as lighting, window sensors, and temperature control. It demonstrates how everyday home tasks can be managed remotely through an app, making life easier and safer.
  • The Smart Farm Kit focuses on agricultural automation. It includes soil moisture sensors, an automatic watering system, and environmental monitoring tools to support plant care without constant human attention.
  • The Solar Station is designed to provide a renewable energy source for the village. It collects and distributes solar energy, helping to reduce reliance on traditional electricity and making the village more sustainable.
Together, these three components work as a complete system, creating a village that is not only smart and convenient but also eco-friendly and future-oriented.

    How this project can be reflected in full-scale building?:

    The "Smart Village" project is designed with simplicity and accessibility in mind, enabling anyone interested in creating a smart home to easily assemble and install the necessary components. By purchasing individual parts from local stores, users can customize their smart home systems at a lower cost compared to pre-packaged solutions. Moreover, building and configuring these systems can be an engaging and educational experience.​
